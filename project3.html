Covid-19 Dashboard in Tableau

Background

This project was inspired by an assignment from my Independent Studies. Please visit my tableau public to view the dashboard.

Objective

The project based on open source data obtained by Hendratno from various official government daily reports of covid-19 cases, recovery, and deaths throughout Indonesia. The goal is to create a visualization of the data, inpired by WHO covid-19 dashboard.

Result

The dashboard consists of

  • Map chart to visualize the cases severity in certain provinces with its ordering,
  • Numerical summary of cases, recovery, and deaths,
  • Daily cases record with its moving average, and
  • Cases break down by island,
alongside the plots and numbers, the dashboard is equipped with filters for dates, provinces, and islands.
