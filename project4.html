Sales Dashboard in Excel

Background

This project is mainly made for this portfolio, to demonstrate my Excel skills that have been accumulated thoughout various courses and projects. This particular dashboard was inspired by two youtube sources, with the structure referencing AlexTheAnalyst along with the dataset, and the visual referencing Kenji Explains. Please visit my github repository to view the dashboard and its inner workings.

Objective

The project based on open source data obtained from the source mentioned above. It contains various customer's background with their bike purchase decision, being purchasing or not purchasing. The goal is to create visulization of the data to identify certain customer traits that results in higher purchase likelihood.

Method and Result

The data is cleaned and molded to suit our visualization goal, using power query techniques. This approach is significantly easier and resilient in comparison to usual formula or macro approach. Then we pull certain information that will form our charts using pivot tables.

The resulting dashboard consists of

  • Total purchase rate,
  • Purchase rate by region,
  • Purchase rate by age bracket,
  • Purchase rate by commute distance, and
  • Average income by gender and purchase decision,
alongside filters for matiral status, home ownership, car ownership, and occupations.
